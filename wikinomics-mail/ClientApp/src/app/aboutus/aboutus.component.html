Vision

Mission

KnowONE is a career platform that empowers you, by connecting you to people who are relevant for you and your career growth.

Through anonymity, we lower the threshold to ask those burning career oriented questions you may have, but are too afraid to ask.

Our mission is to ensure that no talent is wasted due to lack of a relevant network.

Words From The Founder, Mi Le Hagen

Why "KnowONE"?

After 10 years of work experience, I've come across many people with super interesting jobs. And, I've often thought "man, I wish I knew about that profession when I was about to choose my field of study".

On behalf of my younger self, I wish that I could reach out to anyone who had an interesting title, or a job that seemed cool, and ask them: "What did you study? What do you earn? What's the best part about your job? What's the worst part about your job?".

So, I went on a mission to find out how people made their decisions regarding their field of study, or choice of profession. And, after conversations with countless of people, it hit me that the same sentence was repeated over and over:

" I wanted to study engineering. But, I didn't know anyone who is an engineer."
"I became a doctor, because I knew many people who were doctors..."

I realised that; for most of us, our career paths have been highly influenced by people around us- the people within our network. This means that, our source of reference - or, source of information, if you will- is limited by the size of our network.

You're probably thinking: "What about Google?" And, sure, we can Google our way through many of our questions. But, it's not the same as getting answers from real people- people who have experience about

This is the pain-point I discovered. And, this is how the idea of "KnowONE" came about.

Get to know ONE person. And, benefit from the Knowledge, network, and experience of ONE. Because, sometimes that's all it takes.

What Makes KnowONE Different?

We understand that all people wired differently. So, while some people don't mind reaching out to strangers to ask for information, others find this to be extremely uncomfortable.

In today's society, we seem to be catering only to those who are extroverted; those who love to mingle, and make new connections.

At KnowONE we are passionate about making sure that no talent, or potential is wasted, simply because they are too shy, or introverted. So, to remove the fear of reaching out to strangers, we've made sure that you can be anonymous. Because, fear of judgment should not stand in the way of getting the information you need to develop yourself, and your career.
