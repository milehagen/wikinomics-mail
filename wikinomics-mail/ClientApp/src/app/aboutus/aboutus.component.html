Hvem er vi?

1.

We envision a world where zero talent is wasted.

Wikinomics is a platform and community that empowers you, by enabling access to people who study, or work, within the field of your curiosity. By doing so, we help you expand the network that is relevant for you and your career growth.

Our mission is to ensure that no talent is wasted due to lack of a relevant network.

2.

"I'm no one. What can I offer to these experienced people?".

Sound familiar? It might not always feel like it, but everyone has value to offer others. So, you may feel like "no one", but we are convinced that you know (at least) ONE person who can be of value to someone else.

So, more than a career platform, it's a movement.
