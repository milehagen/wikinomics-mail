Legg Alle Antagelser Til Side


Hva gjør en produktdesigner? Hvordan er hverdagen til en såkalt "data scientist"? Hva gjør egentlig en administrerende direktør? Og, hvordan er det å studere "digital kunst"?

KnowONE er stedet der du kan putte alle antagelser rundt studier og karriere til side, og få svar direkte fra de som studerer, eller jobber med det du er nysgjerrig på.

This is the Place Where You Can...


  • Recieve career advice from someone you want to learn from.
  • Share your knowledge and experience with those who seek it.
  • Discuss topics that matter to you and your peers.
  • Join groups and communities that serve as information resources, and cheerleaders for your career development.

A Safe Place For "Stupid" Questions


We believe that there are no "stupid questions". However, we also know how scary it can be to put yourself out there. That's why we've made sure that you can be anonymous.

...Because, fear of judgement should not stand in the way of gaining knowledge.

You Can Make A Difference


It doesn't matter if you're a student, a CEO of a big company, or an aspiring entrepreneur. What you have gone through in life is unique, and someone out there needs to hear what you have to say.

So, in addition to recieving advice and learning from others, we hope you take the time to give back to society, by sharing what you have learnt throughout your career journey.
