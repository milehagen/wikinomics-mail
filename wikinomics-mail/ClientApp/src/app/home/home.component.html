Real People
Real Answers

KnowONE is the go-to-place for your burning career-oriented questions; answered by ordinary people, who study within the field of your interest, hold a role you may be curious about, or work within an industry you want to learn more about.

Put All Your Assumptions Aside

Curious about what a product designer does? How is it really like to work as a data scientist? What does a "CEO" really do? And, what is it really like to study "digital art"?

KnowONE is the place where you can put all your career-related assumptions aside, and get your questions answered straight from the horse's mouth!

This is the Place Where You Can...

  • Receive career advice from someone whose career journey you admire
  • Share your knowledge and experience with those who seek it.
  • Discuss topics that matter to you and your peers.
  • Join groups and communities of practice that serve as information resources, and cheerleaders for your career development.

A Safe Place For "Stupid" Questions

We believe that there are no "stupid questions". However, we also know how scary it can be to put yourself out there. That's why we've made sure that you can be anonymous.

...Because, fear of judgement should not stand in the way of gaining knowledge.

You Are More Powerful than You Think

Think back to your younger self, when you were exploring what to study, day-dreaming about your future job, or working towards a promotion at work.

What do you wish someone told you? What advice would you give to your younger self?

You Can Make A Difference

It doesn't matter if you're a student, a CEO of a big company, or an aspiring entrepreneur. What you have gone through in life is unique, and we believe that there is someone out there who is looking for the information, and experience that you have.

So, in addition to receiving advice and learning from others, we hope you take the time to give back to society, by using KnowONE to share what you have learnt throughout your career journey.
